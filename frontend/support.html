Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with BSFIP Platform?

+

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, complete your business profile, and begin with our financial health assessment. We offer free onboarding sessions for all new users.

Is my data secure and private?

+

Yes, we use bank-level encryption and comply with Botswana's data protection regulations. Your data is never shared with third parties without explicit consent.

Do you offer training in Setswana?

+

Yes! We offer training sessions in both English and Setswana to ensure all Botswana business owners can benefit from our platform.

What if I need help outside business hours?

+

Our comprehensive documentation and video tutorials are available 24/7. For urgent matters, you can submit a support ticket and we'll respond within 24 hours.
