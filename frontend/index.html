About BSFIP Platform

Empowering Botswana's SME Ecosystem

The Botswana SME Financial Intelligence Platform (BSFIP) is a cutting-edge decision support system designed specifically for Small and Medium Enterprises and micro-lenders in Botswana. Developed as part of advanced research in financial technology, BSFIP leverages artificial intelligence and machine learning to provide real-time financial health assessments and risk evaluations.
